Ayushman Bharat Health Data Intelligence Repository (ABHDIR)

Predictive Public Health Analytics for PMJAY & National Health Systems

About the Repository

ABHDIR is a structured health and epidemiological data intelligence platform designed to support Ayushman Bharat and PMJAY initiatives. It enables disease outbreak prediction, longitudinal beneficiary tracking, and AI-driven public health analytics aligned with national healthcare priorities.

Core Capabilities

  • Disease outbreak predictive modeling
  • Longitudinal wellness tracking for PMJAY beneficiaries
  • Epidemiological trend analysis
  • Public health risk forecasting
  • Integration-ready architecture for national dashboards

Strategic Alignment

Designed to align with national digital health objectives and scalable public health infrastructure. Supports data collaboration frameworks and research-oriented integration pathways.

Technology Framework

  • Python, R, and Machine Learning Libraries
  • SQL / PostgreSQL Databases
  • Big Data Processing Frameworks
  • Interactive Data Visualization Tools
